Penalty Structure

• Success rate <95%: MMK 750 penalty per failed delivery

• On-time rate <90%: MMK 375 penalty per late delivery

• Unresolved claims >7 days: MMK 7,500 per day delay

• Persistent underperformance: Contract review and potential termination

Insurance & Liability Coverage

Standard Package (under MMK 150,000)

• Basic coverage: Replacement value up to declared amount

• Maximum coverage: MMK 225,000 per package

• No additional premium required

High-Value Package (over MMK 150,000)

• Comprehensive insurance: 1.5% of declared value

• Full replacement cost up to MMK 750,000 per package

• Enhanced tracking and security protocols

Claims Process & Investigation

• Lost package investigation: Begin within 48 hours

• Resolution timeline: Within 5 business days

• Compensation: Full declared value for confirmed loss/damage
