Executive Summary

Opportunity

Leverage 1M+ active users and 30-year Blazon MM Group reputation to capture untapped market segments through strategic multi-vendor expansion.

Strategy

"Complement, don't compete" – onboard high-quality Yangon-based sellers in gap categories (watches, fragrances, electronics, accessories, wellness).

Operating Model

KHIT ZAY handles platform operations, packaging, delivery logistics, and customer service while sellers focus on product quality and curation.

Phased Plan

Pilot (M1-4) → Controlled Expansion (M5-7) → Full-Scale Launch (M8-12). Quality-over-quantity vendor onboarding strategy.

Financials

Break-even ≈ 215 orders/month. Profitability expected between months 9-12 with net margins reaching 15-20% by year-end.
