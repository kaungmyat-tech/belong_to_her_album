Seller Qualification Framework & Timeline

Qualification Requirements

Essential Requirements

Registered Myanmar business license with physical presence or warehouse in Yangon Region

100% authentic products with proper import documentation

Capacity to fulfill minimum 50 orders per month consistently

Agreement to quality standards and return policies

Product liability insurance for items over MMK 500,000

Quality Standards

100% authentic products with documentation available upon request

No counterfeit, replica, or IP-infringing goods allowed

All items must be new unless clearly marked as refurbished with warranties
