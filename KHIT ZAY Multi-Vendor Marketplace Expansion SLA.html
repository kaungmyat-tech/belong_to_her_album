Service Level Agreement (SLA)

1. Executive Summary & Purpose

This Service Level Agreement (SLA) outlines the terms, responsibilities, and performance metrics governing the relationship between Blazon MM Group (operating as "KHIT ZAY") and its authorized Marketplace Sellers and Logistics Partners. This agreement is designed to ensure the highest standards of service for KHIT ZAY's 1M+ active customers while facilitating a "Complement, Don't Compete" expansion strategy.

The core objective is to maintain premium positioning while expanding into Watches, Authentic Fragrances, Small Electronics, Fashion Accessories, and Home & Wellness categories.

2. Seller Service Level Agreement

2.1 Seller Qualification & Compliance

All prospective sellers must adhere to the following strict qualification criteria to maintain platform integrity:

  • Business License: Must hold a valid registered business license in Myanmar.
  • Physical Presence: Must maintain a physical office or warehouse within the Yangon Region for logistics coordination.
  • Product Authenticity: 100% guarantee of product authenticity. Counterfeit, replica, or IP-infringing goods are strictly prohibited. Violations result in immediate termination and potential legal action (fines up to 5x value of goods).
  • Capacity: Demonstrated ability to fulfill a minimum of 50 orders per month.

2.3 Commission Structure & Tiers

Commission rates are determined by the seller's monthly order volume and operational phase.

Phased Launch Rates:
  • Pilot Phase (Months 1-4): 8% Flat Commission (Incentive Rate)
  • Growth Phase (Months 5-8): 12% Standard Commission

Mature Phase (Month 9+) Performance Tiers:

Tier Monthly Orders Commission Additional Benefits
Bronze 0 - 50 12% Standard support, Basic dashboard
Silver 51 - 150 10% Priority support, 1 free feature placement/month
Gold 151+ 8% Dedicated manager, 2 free feature placements, Early feature access

2.4 Seller Return & Refund Policy

  • Return Window: 7 days from delivery for defects, wrong items, or description mismatches.
  • Cost Allocation: Seller bears shipping costs for seller-fault returns; Customer bears costs for preference-based returns.
  • Processing Speed: Returns must be inspected within 48 hours of receipt. Refunds must be processed within 3 business days of approval.
  • Penalty: Delayed refunds (>5 days) incur MMK 7,500 per day penalty.

3. Delivery Partner Service Level Agreement

3.1 Delivery Timeframes

Delivery partners (e.g., J&T Express, Myanmar Post EMS, DHL) commit to the following service standards:

Major Cities (Mandalay/Naypyidaw) 2-3 Business Days 1-2 Business Days
Other Townships 3-7 Business Days 2-5 Business Days

3.2 Logistics KPIs & Penalties

Partners are measured against the following Key Performance Indicators (KPIs). Repeated failure to meet these targets will result in contract review.

  • Successful Delivery Rate: Target 97% minimum.
    Penalty: MMK 750 per failed delivery below 95%.
  • On-Time Delivery Rate: Target 95% minimum.
    Penalty: MMK 375 per late delivery below 90%.
  • Pickup Success Rate: Target 98% minimum. Pickups must occur within scheduled morning (9-12) or afternoon (2-5) windows.
  • Claim Resolution: Must be resolved within 5 business days.
    Penalty: MMK 7,500 per day delay beyond 7 days.

3.3 Insurance & Liability

  • Standard Coverage (Items < MMK 150,000): Coverage up to declared value (Max MMK 225,000).
  • High-Value Coverage (Items > MMK 150,000): Comprehensive insurance at 1.5% of value, covering full replacement cost up to MMK 750,000.
  • Loss/Damage Investigation: Must initiate within 48 hours of report.

4. Platform Performance Standards

4.1 System Availability & Support

KHIT ZAY (Blazon MM Group) commits to providing a robust technical infrastructure to support all marketplace transactions.

  • Uptime Guarantee: 99.9% platform uptime via Shopify Plus infrastructure.
  • Seller Portal Access: 24/7 access to inventory and order management systems.
  • Technical Support: Dedicated technical support specialist available 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (MMT).

4.2 Financial Settlement

KHIT ZAY guarantees transparent financial handling for all sellers:

  • Settlement Cycle: Bi-weekly disbursement of net revenue (Sales minus Commission and Fees).
  • Reporting: Detailed transaction statements provided 2 days before disbursement.
